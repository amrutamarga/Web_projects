President’s Message

President’s Message

It is indeed heartwarming to note that most of our collective goals, aspirations, and dreams that we had envisaged about ‘community well-being’ years ago have translated into success stories. It is all due to the efforts of our Associates, Affiliates, and a countless number of people achieving their health goals since our establishment nearly four decades ago.


It is also worth noting that most of our students have embarked on full-fledged/lucrative careers in Yoga. They are spreading and spearheading the ancient wisdom as a  way of life besides owning center[s] upon graduation from our yoga courses worldwide.


In the past seven years, we have consistently met our objectives of serving the cause of Yoga by demonstrating the highest standard of teaching and providing all the necessary support.  The result is that many instructors who graduated from our University continue to enjoy being our affiliates and associates worldwide, what’s more! The students have also accredited themselves to be members of the Yoga Certification Board of the Ministry of AYUSH ( YCB) also become members of the Indian Yoga Association ( IYA), Asian Yoga Therapists of Association (AYTA) International association based in Singapore.


All other Yoga Instructors and centers not currently teaching our Yoga modules and programs or indirect association with us are welcome to join hands and become VYASA, Affiliates in India, and as VYASA.Associates at International levels.


The year 2014 will go down in Vyasa / S Vyasa history as 0ne of the most significant years. We have officially labeled Yoga programs developed by our University Research Centre as ‘Vivekananda Yoga.’ 


We warmly welcome each of you to join our global net work and continue the journey to learn, practice, teach, and own our Yoga programs.   The coordinated efforts will continue to benefit the society at large, especially in overcoming the challenging times that all of us are in;


This is an open invitation to all of you to be a part of this magnificent endeavor of rebuilding a healthier society around us. 


Sincerly,

Dr. H. R. Nagendra

President - VYASA

Chancellor - S-VYASA
